\#\# 2\) What Concierge Is (and isn’t)  
\#\#\# 2.1 Quick definition  
\*\*Headline:\*\* A friend in every city—without the awkward guesswork    
\*\*Body:\*\*  
Concierge is our premium planning layer. Tell us what you want, what you’re worried about, and how you like to travel. We’ll recommend vetted guides, build a plan around your vibe, and help you book confidently.

\#\#\# 2.2 What we help with (bullets)  
\- Curated itinerary suggestions (culture / food / nightlife / queer history)  
\- Safety-first guidance for LGBTQ+ travelers (practical, not fear-based)  
\- Matching you with the right verified guide(s)  
\- Restaurant and neighborhood recommendations  
\- Coordination notes: meeting points, timing, and “what to expect”  
\- Optional: multi-city or longer trips (handled manually)

\#\#\# 2.3 What we don’t do (clear boundaries)  
\- We don’t provide legal advice or guarantee safety outcomes  
\- We don’t arrange sexual services or “dates”  
\- We don’t book flights/hotels directly (yet) — we recommend and guide you

\---

\#\# 3\) How it works (simple 3 steps)  
\*\*Step 1 — Tell us your trip\*\*  
City/cities, dates, interests, budget range, and anything you’re concerned about.

\*\*Step 2 — We propose a plan\*\*  
A curated shortlist of guide(s) \+ suggested itinerary structure and safety notes.

\*\*Step 3 — You book with confidence\*\*  
We help you confirm details and book through Rainbow Tour Guides (or give you a clear next action plan).

\#\# 9\) FAQ (reduce objections)  
1\) \*\*Do I need an account?\*\*    
No. But you’ll need one to book guides and manage messages.

2\) \*\*Is concierge available for every city?\*\*    
We prioritize cities where we have verified guides. If we can’t support your destination, we’ll tell you quickly.

3\) \*\*Can you help me choose the right guide?\*\*    
Yes—matching is a core part of concierge.

4\) \*\*Do you guarantee safety?\*\*    
We don’t guarantee outcomes, but we design for safety: verified guides, clear standards, and escalation paths.

5\) \*\*Can you plan nightlife?\*\*    
Yes—if that’s your vibe. Always with clear boundaries and safety-first guidance.
